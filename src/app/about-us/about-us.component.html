About Us

With technical expertise, design department and marketing from Europe and by making use of the growing facilities in the East, we are supplying world class textile products not only with a quality to be rated amongst the best, but at very affordable prices.

Our European team with vast experience in textile production and trade, gives direction to our business keeping in mind the prevailing and future European market trends. Our Designers project consumer demand according to ever changing demand in Europe so that our production team incorporate the same in the final product.
